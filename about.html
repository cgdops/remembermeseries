About the Founder

After a successful career in the movie business, working on over 65 films including Cape Fear, Friday Night Lights, Ace Ventura: Pet Detective, and Steel Magnolias, Bill McConnell felt called to tell a different kind of story. A chance moment of reflection revealed just how many people carry silent struggles, longing to know they’re seen, loved, and not forgotten.

Drawing on his decades of experience in filmmaking, Bill created the Remember Me series—powerful, intimate micro dramas that speak to the hearts of people walking through pain, doubt, or loneliness. His vision is to reach audiences around the world with these timeless stories, offering encouragement and reminding each viewer that even in their darkest moments, their story still matters.

Through these short, evocative films, Bill aims to help individuals feel understood and inspired to keep going—because someone sees them, and their life has meaning.
